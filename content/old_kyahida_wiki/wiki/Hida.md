**Hida** is the continent facing [Rael](/content/kyahida_wiki/wiki/Rael), and is divided into two regions: [Kyáhida](/content/kyahida_wiki/wiki/Kyáhida) and [Esoenska](/content/kyahida_wiki/wiki/Esoenska).
